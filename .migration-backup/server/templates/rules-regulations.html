📋 These rules apply to all owners, tenants, occupants, and guests within Lakeview Commons. Owners are responsible for ensuring their tenants and guests comply with all community standards.
🌿
Landscaping & Exterior
Rule 1.1
Lawn Maintenance
All lawn areas must be mowed and edged at least once every fourteen (14) days from April through October, and once every thirty (30) days from November through March. Grass shall not exceed four (4) inches in height. Dead or dying grass must be re-seeded or replaced within sixty (60) days.
Rule 1.2
Seasonal Landscaping Required
A minimum of three (3) ornamental shrubs or seasonal plants must be maintained in good condition in the front yard planting bed at all times. Artificial plants are not permitted in any exterior visible location. Mulching of beds is encouraged and must be replenished when depleted below 2-inch depth.
Rule 1.3
Tree Trimming
Trees must be trimmed to prevent overhang onto neighboring properties or obstruction of sidewalks and driveways. Dead branches greater than one (1) inch in diameter must be removed within sixty (60) days. Work on trees taller than 15 feet or with a trunk diameter exceeding 6 inches requires ARC notification before commencement.
Rule 1.4
Vegetable Gardens
Vegetable gardens are permitted only in rear yards and side yards not visible from the street. Front yard vegetable gardens are prohibited. All gardens must be contained within defined bed areas and maintained in a neat, weed-free condition.
Rule 1.5
Holiday Decorations
Exterior holiday decorations may be displayed no more than forty-five (45) days before the associated holiday and must be removed within fifteen (15) days after the holiday. Lighting must be turned off between the hours of 11:00 PM and 6:00 AM. Decorations must not obstruct traffic lines of sight.
🚗
Parking Regulations
Rule 2.1
Assigned Spaces Fines Apply
Each unit is assigned one (1) dedicated parking space as specified in the unit deed or lease. Vehicles must be parked in the unit's assigned space only. Parking in any other assigned space without written permission from the space holder and the Association is prohibited and subject to towing at vehicle owner's expense.
Rule 2.2
Guest Parking
Guest parking spaces are available on a first-come, first-served basis and are limited to 72 consecutive hours per vehicle per visit. Guests staying longer must use street parking or obtain a temporary permit from the management office. A maximum of two (2) guest parking passes per unit may be active simultaneously.
Rule 2.3
Commercial Vehicles
Commercial vehicles (box trucks, vehicles with visible commercial markings, trailers, RVs, and boats) may not be parked in resident or guest parking areas overnight (between 9:00 PM and 6:00 AM). Temporary exceptions for up to 48 hours for active construction or moving may be requested from management.
Rule 2.4
Vehicle Maintenance
No vehicle maintenance, repair, or restoration work (other than emergency flat tire changes) may be performed in parking areas, driveways, or common areas. Vehicles must be in operable condition; vehicles that are inoperable, unregistered, or missing tires or windows may be towed after a 72-hour notice period.
Rule 2.5
Speed Limit
The maximum speed within all Lakeview Commons roadways and parking areas is 10 miles per hour. Pedestrians, bicyclists, and children always have the right of way. Drivers must stop completely at all marked stop signs within the community.
🔊
Noise & Quiet Hours
Rule 3.1
Quiet Hours Fines Apply
Quiet hours are observed daily from 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM (Monday–Friday) and from 11:00 PM to 8:00 AM (Saturday–Sunday). During quiet hours, all noise emanating from units must be maintained at a level that cannot be heard in adjacent units with interior doors and windows closed.
Rule 3.2
Construction and Power Equipment
Exterior construction, landscaping equipment (lawn mowers, leaf blowers, etc.), and power tools may only be operated Monday through Friday, 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM, and Saturday, 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM. No such equipment may be operated on Sundays or federal holidays without advance Board approval.
Rule 3.3
Parties and Gatherings
Gatherings of more than fifteen (15) persons at a unit are considered events and require notification to the management office at least 48 hours in advance. The unit owner is responsible for ensuring all guests comply with quiet hours and parking regulations. Music must not be audible from the property line after 9:00 PM.
Rule 3.4
Pets and Animals
Persistent animal noise (defined as barking, howling, or vocalizing lasting more than fifteen (15) continuous minutes or occurring intermittently for more than thirty (30) minutes within a one-hour period) constitutes a noise violation. Owners are responsible for taking corrective action when notified of noise complaints.
🐾
Pet Policies
Rule 4.1
Registration Required
All pets must be registered with the management office within fourteen (14) days of occupancy or acquisition. Registration requires proof of current vaccinations and spay/neuter status. An annual registration fee of $50 per pet applies. Maximum of two (2) pets per unit (cats and dogs combined).
Rule 4.2
Leash Requirement Fines Apply
All dogs must be on a leash no longer than six (6) feet when in any common area. Dogs are not permitted in the pool area, fitness center, or building lobbies (except service animals). Owners must maintain physical control of the leash at all times.
Rule 4.3
Waste Cleanup $50 Fine
Pet owners must immediately collect and properly dispose of all pet waste in designated waste stations throughout the community. Failure to clean up after a pet is subject to an immediate $50 fine. Waste stations are located at each parking lot entrance and along all walking paths.
Rule 4.4
Breed Restrictions
The following breeds and breed mixes require special insurance documentation (minimum $100,000 per occurrence) and written Board approval before occupancy: Pit Bull Terrier, Rottweiler, Doberman Pinscher, Chow Chow, and Akita. Approval is at the Board's sole discretion.
🏊
Common Areas & Amenities
Rule 5.1
Pool Hours & Rules
The community pool is open daily from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM (Memorial Day through Labor Day) and 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM (September 1 through May 25). Children under age 14 must be accompanied by a resident adult. No glass containers, alcohol, or food within the pool barrier fence. Maximum occupancy: 40 persons.
Rule 5.2
Fitness Center
The fitness center is available to residents 24/7 using key fob access. Proper workout attire and closed-toe athletic shoes required at all times. Equipment must be wiped down after use. Children under 16 are not permitted. Maximum occupancy: 12 persons. Guests are permitted (max 1 per resident).
Rule 5.3
Clubhouse Reservations
The community clubhouse may be reserved for private events by residents in good standing. Reservations must be made at least seven (7) days in advance with a $200 refundable deposit. Maximum capacity: 75 persons. Events must conclude by 11:00 PM. The resident is responsible for cleanup and any damages.
Rule 5.4
Trash & Recycling
Trash and recyclables must be placed in designated receptacles only. Bulk items (furniture, appliances, mattresses) require a special pickup appointment — do not leave in common areas. Trash bins may only be placed at the designated collection area the evening before and must be retrieved by 8:00 PM on collection day.
🏠
Leasing & Tenants
Rule 6.1
Lease Notification Required
Owners who lease their units must notify the management office in writing within five (5) business days of a new tenancy. Notification must include the tenant's name(s), contact information, and lease term. Owners remain responsible for all HOA assessments and for ensuring tenant compliance with all Rules and Regulations.
Rule 6.2
Minimum Lease Term
Short-term rentals (Airbnb, VRBO, etc.) and leases with terms of less than ninety (90) days are strictly prohibited. All leases must be in writing. Violations of this rule are subject to fines of $500 per occurrence per day and may result in referral for legal action.
Rule 6.3
Rental Caps
A maximum of twenty percent (20%) of total units may be rented at any one time. When the rental cap has been reached, no new leases may commence until the percentage of rented units falls below 20%. Owners intending to rent must confirm rental availability with the management office before executing a lease.
